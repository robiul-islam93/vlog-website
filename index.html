Tour de Tea Garden, Sylhet

Many of us can't imagine the morning without a cup of smoking tea. If you want to enjoy the mind-blowing view of tea gardens across the horizon, visit the Sylhet division. Because, out of the total 167 tea gardens in Bangladesh, 135 are located in greater Sylhet. Besides Sylhet, there are tea gardens in some other districts like Panchuagar and Chittagong. The collection of the tea leaves starts in May and lasts till October. That’s why this is the best time to see a tea garden full of greenery.
